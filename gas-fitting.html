Our Services

Gas Fitting

Licensed gas technicians for safe, code-compliant gas line installation and appliance hookups.

Licensed Gas Work

Gas work requires a licensed technician - there's no room for error. Our gas fitters are certified and experienced in residential gas systems, from simple appliance hookups to complete gas line installations.

We handle natural gas and propane systems, always following code requirements and safety protocols.

Gas Appliance Installation

Upgrading to a gas range? Adding a tankless water heater? Installing a gas fireplace? We run the gas lines and make the connections to get your new equipment up and running safely.

Proper sizing, pressure testing, and leak checking on every job.

  • Gas range and cooktop connections

  • Outdoor gas lines for BBQs

Safety First

Gas work is regulated for good reason. Improper installation can lead to leaks, carbon monoxide exposure, or worse. We pull permits where required, follow manufacturer specifications, and test every connection before leaving the job. Your safety is non-negotiable.

When You Need Help Fast

Some plumbing problems can't wait until Monday. A burst pipe, a flooded basement, or a gas leak needs immediate attention to prevent damage, protect your family, and get your home back to normal.

We provide emergency plumbing service throughout Simcoe County. When you call, you talk to us directly - not an answering service.

Burst Pipes

Water damage adds up fast. We'll locate the break, stop the water, and make repairs to get you back up and running.

Flooding & Water Damage

Basement flooding, appliance failures, or sump pump issues. Quick response to minimize damage to your home.

No Hot Water

Water heater failed? We diagnose the problem and repair or replace your unit to restore hot water quickly.

Sewer Backups

A backed-up sewer is a health hazard. We clear blockages and get your drains flowing properly again.

Gas Leaks

If you smell gas, leave immediately and call us. Licensed gas fitters to locate and repair gas leaks safely.

Frozen Pipes

Frozen pipes can burst when they thaw. We safely thaw pipes and repair any damage before it gets worse.

While You Wait: What To Do

  1. Shut off the water. Find your main shut-off valve (usually near the water meter) and turn it off to stop flooding.
  2. If you smell gas, leave the house. Don't flip switches or use phones inside. Call from outside or a neighbor's house.
  3. Move valuables away from water. If safe to do so, get furniture, electronics, and belongings away from flooded areas.
  4. Document the damage. Take photos for insurance purposes before cleanup begins.
